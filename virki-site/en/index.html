VIRKI was built to operate where conventional solutions do not reach. Our team brings together professionals from investment banks, credit managers and M&A boutiques, combining strategic perspective with technical execution in transactions that demand precision and speed.

Our edge is the ability to structure from scratch: we identify the right instrument, map the relevant investors and run the process from diagnosis to close.

"Liquidity is not a product. It is the result of a well-designed structure."

We work on advisory mandates that require confidentiality, speed and deep knowledge of the Brazilian financial market. We understand both the asset originator and the investor who needs to price the risk. VIRKI creates value precisely at that intersection.

Three fronts. One
goal: liquidity.

Every mandate is built to specification. We do not offer off-the-shelf solutions. We deliver precise diagnosis and execution driven by the client's outcome.

Structured Credit

Structured Credit Facilities

We design structured debt instruments (FIDCs, CRIs, CRAs, debentures and custom transactions) for companies that need capital without dilution. We advise on every stage, from structuring to issuance.

M&A

M&A and Asset Monetization

We run sale, merger and acquisition processes in special situations: companies under restructuring, non-core assets, spin-offs and strategic divestitures. We map qualified buyers and follow through to close.

NPLs

NPL Portfolio Sales

We advise creditors on the assignment of performing and non-performing loan portfolios. We price the portfolio, prepare the data room and run the process to close, with value recovery as the priority.

From diagnosis to close.

I

Diagnosis

We map the asset, the corporate structure, liabilities involved and regulatory constraints. We define the right instrument before any move in the market.

II

Structuring

We design the transaction with full legal, financial and tax documentation. Every structure is built to maximize the client's return and minimize unnecessary risk exposure.

III

Distribution

We access our network of institutional investors, family offices, FIDCs and specialized NPL buyers. We run the process with discretion and efficiency, preserving the negotiated value.

IV

Close

We accompany due diligence, final negotiation and signing. The mandate does not end with the offer. It ends with funds in the account and the asset transferred.
